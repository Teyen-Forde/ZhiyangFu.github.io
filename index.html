Zhiyang Fu

I earned my Ph.D. in Electrical & Computer Engineering with a minor in Optical Science at the University of Arizona in November, 2021. My dissertation work "Supervised and Self-supervised Learning for Accelerated Medical Imaging" was under the supervision of Prof. Ali Bilgin and Prof. Maria I. Altbach. My research interest lies in the intersection of machine learning and medical imaging. I am also interested in artifacts correction in medical imaging.
